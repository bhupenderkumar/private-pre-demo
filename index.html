Why Families Choose Us

Private Pre — The Difference

Discover why families across Austin trust us with their children's education.

👩‍🏫

Expert Faculty

Our teachers are highly qualified, experienced, and passionate about education. We offer a low student-to-teacher ratio to ensure personalized attention and support.

👥

Small Classes

We believe that small class sizes allow for meaningful relationships, active participation, and effective learning. Our classes are designed to foster a sense of community and belonging.

🚪

Safe Campus

Our campus is designed with safety in mind. We have secure entrances, surveillance cameras, and a dedicated staff to ensure our students' well-being.

📚

Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to meet the needs of the 21st-century learner. We offer a range of subjects, including STEM, arts, and foreign languages, to equip our students for success.

🌈

Whole-Child Education

We believe that education should encompass the whole child, including social, emotional, and physical development. Our programs promote character growth, creativity, and critical thinking skills.

🌟

Community

At Private Pre, we value community and believe that it plays a vital role in our students' education. We offer a range of programs and activities that foster relationships, teamwork, and social responsibility.

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for children aged 3-4 and focuses on exploration, creativity, and socialization. Our experienced teachers create a nurturing environment that fosters a love of learning.

📚

Elementary

Our elementary program for grades K-5 emphasizes a balanced curriculum that includes reading, writing, mathematics, science, and social studies. We also offer art, music, and physical education to promote creativity and physical fitness.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our middle and upper school programs for grades 6-8 build on the foundation established in our elementary program. We offer a range of electives, including STEM, arts, and foreign languages, to prepare our students for success in high school and beyond.
