Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for children aged 3-5 and focuses on play-based learning, socialization, and early literacy skills.

📚

Elementary

In our elementary program, students learn through hands-on activities, projects, and experiments that promote curiosity and a love of learning.

📝

Middle/Upper

Our middle and upper school programs offer a challenging and supportive environment where students can explore their interests and develop important skills for high school and beyond.

💻

STEM

Our STEM program teaches students essential skills in science, technology, engineering, and math through hands-on projects, experiments, and coding activities.

🎨

Arts

Our arts program offers a range of courses and activities in music, visual arts, drama, and dance, helping students develop their creativity and self-expression.

🏈

Athletics

Our athletic program provides opportunities for students to participate in team sports, develop their physical skills, and learn important values like teamwork and sportsmanship.
