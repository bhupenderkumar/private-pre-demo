Our Mission & History

Founded in 2010, Private Pre was established to provide a high-quality, personalized education to families in Austin. Our small class sizes and experienced teachers allow us to tailor our instruction to meet the needs of every student.

At Private Pre, we believe that learning should be a joyous and engaging experience. Our teachers use innovative methods and hands-on activities to bring subjects to life and inspire a love of learning in our students.

In addition to academic excellence, we also place a strong emphasis on character development, community service, and extracurricular activities. Our students have numerous opportunities to participate in sports, music, and art programs, as well as volunteer work and leadership initiatives.
