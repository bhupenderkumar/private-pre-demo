Our Mission & History

With a rich history of academic excellence, Private Pre Austin has been serving the Austin community for over a decade. Our school was founded on the belief that every child deserves a high-quality education that prepares them for success in college and beyond.

At Private Pre, we believe in a balanced approach to learning that combines academic rigor with creative exploration and character development. Our dedicated teachers use a variety of teaching methods to engage students and foster a love of learning that extends beyond the classroom.

From art and music to sports and clubs, we offer a range of extracurricular activities that help students develop new skills, build friendships, and explore their passions. Our goal is to create a well-rounded individual who is prepared to make a positive impact in the world.
